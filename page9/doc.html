The Aims of The Website

Creating a website for our bakery is a cost-effective way of expanding our business. Taking oour services online enables visitors to purchase oour baked goods through the internet. People are now more inclined to make purchases online because of the convenience it offers. By accepting online orders, we are making our products more accessible to customers who may not be able to drive down to our physical store

The Intended Audience for The Website

Sim's Cake Town will appeal to all households and residents. Whether someone is working class, a business executive, a teacher, or a student, there will always be a need for a bakery. Younger customers may order pastries from a bakery, while families may drop by for breads and rolls. Orders for tiered cakes for birthdays and other special events may come from high-income households
